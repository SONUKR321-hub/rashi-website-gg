Poster / Paper Presentation

AI powered genomics : Revolutionizing personalized Healthcare Through Telemedicine

Our team of five—Rashi Agrawal, Deepali Singh, Astuti Kumari, Atri Ghosh, Spandan Bhattacharjee, —participated in the poster presentation challenge with our project on ''AI-Powered Genomics: Revolutionizing Personalized Healthcare Through Telemedicine". We demonstrated the impact of AI in genetic engineering through collaborative research and creative presentation. This experience showcased our teamwork and research skills.
